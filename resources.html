Dictionary

Some MGFX’s common terminology.

Composite

To combine images or sequences to make a singular picture.

Cut

To switch from one clip to another.

Effect

The result of processing audio and video clips to enhance, improve or distort them.

Keyframe

A point along a timeline that specifies where and how the settings for an effect will change.

Layering

Adding multiple layers of superimposed video.

Mask

Used to define the transparent areas of each frame.

Scale

Reduce or enlarge an image or video by alterting the entire image.

Sequence

Images or footage that follow each other in a order.

Transition

How one shot changes to the next.
