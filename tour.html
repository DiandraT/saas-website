MGFX made it’s start in Ottawa, Canada.

It is a software program used to create, composite, stylize footage and add visual effects to videos for film, tv, and video games. It is a visual effects, motion graphics and compositing software developed for post-production purposes. It can also be used for keying, tracking, animation and more.
