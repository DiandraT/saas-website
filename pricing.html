FAQ

How long are your subscriptions

MGFX offers plans that are paid monthly or yearly. We make it simple to start and stop your subscription.

Is there a discount for yearly services?

Yes, MGFX offers yearly discounts and online giftcards to members who have shown yearly and continuous commitments.

Is there a discount for monthly services?

No, MGFX only offers discounts and online gift cards to users who has signed up with a yearly plan.

Do you offer student pricing?

No, MGFX does not offer student pricing as our prices are already reasonable for all.

How do I sign up?

You can start with a free trial, but if you want to skip that process click on a subscription button that is seen under the offered plans.

Can I switch plans?

Yes, MGFX allows you to switch plans at any time. Contact us by telephone or email.

How do I cancel the subscription?

Cancelling MGFX is a simple task. You can find how to cancel it under the setting panel within the software. But cancelling requires an annual commitment.

Does MGFX offer any other services?

No, MGFX is primarily a video effect software. Therefore, we focus just on that to deliver the best.
